One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begin re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm Consequently, he becomes acquainted with this inner city Streets, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well writeten and interresting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm , The Pelican Brief, and The Client
